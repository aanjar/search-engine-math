How to Use UM Math Search Engine

Basic Search

To perform a basic search:

  1. Enter your search query in the search box
  2. Click the "Cari" button or press Enter
  3. Browse through the search results

Understanding Search Results

Each search result shows:

  • Title of the page
  • URL of the page
  • A snippet of the content
  • PageRank score (PR)

Viewing Page Details

To view detailed information about a page:

  1. Click on any search result card
  2. View the full content and outbound links
  3. Use the "← Kembali" button to return to search results

Tips for Better Results

  • Use specific mathematical terms
  • Include relevant symbols or formulas
  • Try different variations of your search terms
  • Check the PageRank score for result quality
